Natural Images

Pretraining

ImageNet

The ImageNet dataset contains images organized according to the WordNet hierarchy. This split of the dataset includes images from 1,000 different classes including "goldfish," "forklift," and "burrito."

Transfer

Describable Textures Dataset

The Describable Textures Database contains 5640 images organized according to a list of 47 categories related to human perception of textures, including "banded," "crosshatched," and "knitted." TThe database includes images taken from Google and Flickr where at least 90% of the surface contains a certain category attribute. There are 120 images for each category, ranging in size from 300x300 to 640x640.

FGVC-Aircraft

The FGVC-Aircraft database is comprised of 10,000 images of aircraft grouped into 100 different classes (the aircraft variants, e.g., Boeing 737-700).

Caltech-UCSD Birds

The Caltech-UCSD Birds database consists of images of 200, mostly North American, bird species (e.g. "Pacific Loon," "Mallard")

German Traffic Sign Recognition Benchmark

The German Traffic Sign Recognition Benchmark database contains over 50,000 images of traffic signs in more than 40 classes. Images vary in illumination and contain rich backgrounds.

Oxford_Flowers102

The Oxford_Flowers102 dataset consists of images of flowers from 102 classes commonly occurring in the United Kingdom. The images are varied in scale, pose, and lighting.
